## Selected Questions

1. What proportion of your tickets relate to account access? (Volume probe)
2. Walk me through the last password-reset ticket you handled, step by step. (Process discovery)
3. What do users try before raising a ticket? (Pain points)
4. What happens on your worst day for ticket volume? (NFR probe — volume)
5. If we could remove one category of ticket this quarter, which one? (Prioritization)
